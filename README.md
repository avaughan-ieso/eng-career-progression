# Engineering Careeer Progression

In terms of competency, theory is not enough. Levels are built on relevant working experience as well as theoretical knowledge.

## What is it?
A career progression framework for all roles within Engineering

## Dimensions
- Technical
- Approach and Impact
- Communication and Leadership

## Why?
- We need a way to explain the difference between levels, and give someone guidance on which skills to develop
- Interviewing - objective criteria
- Benchmarking
- How well people are meeting their current obligations
- Developing skills to grow career

## Anti-patterns
Lists of roles and responsibilities - something employees use as a checklist
