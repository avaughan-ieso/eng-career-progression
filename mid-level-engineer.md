# Mid-level Engineer

## Essence
Impact: Unblocks others at the team level. Spends time enhancing their own skills.
Scope: Tackles bigger, more complex stories; occasionally with support. Architecting or designing solutions at a story level.

## Technical
- Is competent to proficient in the primary technology of their discipline (e.g. C#, JavaScript)
- Makes informed decisions about which tools and algorithms to use for specific problems
- Demonstrates competent problem solving skills (debugging, analysis, instrumentation)
- Keeps availability and robustness in mind day to day
- Complies to local coding guidelines and writes code that others understand
- Makes architecture decisions within a system/module/service
- Actively builds up knowledge of neighbouring systems
- Understands trade-offs between testing approaches

## Approach and Impact
- Drives simple epics (an iteration long)
- Understands trade-offs to deliver (MVP mindset)
- Provides reliable story estimates and breaks up the bigger ones
- Is actively involved in the development process end to end
- Finishes or hands off work before leaving for a planned absence
- Keeps track of tech or conceptual debt
- Identifies risks in a project
- Holds themself and the team accountable for removing delivery blockers (e.g. green build)
- Thinks critically and makes informed decisions based on first principles
- Learns from failure: admits mistakes and shares learnings
- Aware of and actively works on their gaps in relevant tech skills
- Selects tasks based on team priorities (not selective/picky)
- Proactively picks up things without an owner
- Refers to goals and metrics in team discussions
- Involves all stakeholders in refining a project's requirements

## Communication and Leadership
- Helps others with pair programming and doing code reviews
- Comments on RFCs, requirements, documentation where the team is a stakeholder
- Can onboard new team members
- Has a basic understanding of all projects and operations of the team
- Suggests process improvements (active in retrospective meetings)
- Networks outside of the company (e.g. going to conferences/meetups)
- Understands the importance of and participates in the hiring process
- Helps the team stay focused
- Can explain and advocate for a certain decision/solution
- Can represent the team (questions, meetings)
- Listens to others and shows empathy - accepts different perspectives
- Provides constructive feedback regularly
- Actively asks for feedback and acts on it
- Actively contributes to team meetings
